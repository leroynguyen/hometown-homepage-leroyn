Must-do Activities

CN Tower
Edgewalk

CN Tower EdgeWalk is an exhilarating experience that allows you to walk hands-free around the top of Toronto's iconic tower, 356 meters above the ground, offering breathtaking 360-degree views of the city and Lake Ontario.

Dundas Square

Dundas Square is the vibrant heart of downtown Toronto, offering live events, street performances, and a bustling atmosphere surrounded by bright billboards and lively crowds.

Canada's
Wonderland

Canada's Wonderland is a thrilling amusement park near Toronto, featuring exhilarating rollercoasters, family-friendly rides, and a refreshing water park, providing fun and excitement for all ages.
